Meet Ms. Carver

Ms. Carver is a freelance CKV art teacher who has been teaching art to students for over 10 years. She has a Bachelor of Arts degree in Art Education from the University of Amsterdam and a Master of Fine Arts degree in Visual Arts from the University of the Arts Amsterdam. She offers a variety of art classes for students in grades 1-12, including drawing, painting, and sculpture. In addition to teaching art, Ms. Carver is also an artist and has exhibited her work in galleries and museums around the world. She is passionate about helping students develop their creativity and artistic skills through fun and engaging art lessons.
